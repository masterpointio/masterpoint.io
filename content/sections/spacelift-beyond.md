### Beyond <span class="text-gradient">Spacelift</span> {.sectionTitle .big .text-pine .pb-0}

Spacelift orchestrates your infrastructure — someone still has to build that infrastructure well. Most of our Spacelift engagements include the platform work underneath.
{.font22 .text-pine .pt-3}

For [Ninjio](https://ninjio.com/), alongside our partners at [MetaCTO](https://www.metacto.com/), we modernized a manually managed EC2 estate into serverless ECS Fargate — dozens of services rebuilt in OpenTofu, environment provisioning down from a month to days, staging costs down 80% — all automated through Spacelift.
{.font20 .text-pine}
